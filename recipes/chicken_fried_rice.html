Chicken Fried Rice

The chicken fried rice is a kind of celebratory dish for most families in Africa; irrespective of social class and financial status. Most occasion: wedding ceremony, traditional marriage rites and parties in generate will always go with the chicken fried rice. It is flavorful and filled with textures from crisp vegetable to super tasty lumps of fried chicken. I funly call it "African fried rice" because it is being prepared across the continent of Africa with almost the same ingredients; the only difference being the way and manner the chef processes it.

Ingredients

  • Parboiled rice
  • Curry powder
  • Thyme
  • Bay leaf
  • Carrot
  • Farrow peas
  • Red kidney beans
  • Sweet corn
  • Chicken
  • Beef liver
  • Onions
  • White pepper
  • Ginger
  • garlic
  • Vegetable oil
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Boil the parboiled rice with seasoning in a pot. Ensure that the rice is completely enveloped by the water.
  2. Prepare the chicken for steaming, add very little water along with some seasoning to saute the chicken. The chicken should be allowed to boil for about 10 minutes and then remove it from the pot.
  3. Chop your beef liver to small size, saute it as you did to the chicken above and remove it from the pot aswell.
  4. Add some onions into the vegetable oil, cook it for about 5 minutes. Add some garlic and ginger so the spices can blossom in the oil.
  5. Add some vegetables to the sauteed onions and fry the mix for about 5 minutes.
  6. You can now add the other vegetables: farrow peas, red kidney beans, sweet corn, carrot.
  7. Add rice to the pot and stir.
  8. At this stage, you can add the seasoned chicken and liver to the pot and stir it.
  9. Finally, get some of the seasoned chicken and place it in a pot fof vegetable oil and fry it until the chicken's color has turn brownish. There you have it: your chicken fried rice is ready to be served!
